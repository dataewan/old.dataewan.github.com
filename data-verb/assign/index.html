assign lets you put new columns on the dataframe, or assign new values to the old ones.

This example makes a new column in the dataframe avgsegment by dividing one column land_border_length in the dataframe by another number_borders.

(
    clean
    .assign(avgsegment = clean.land_border_length / clean.number_borders)
)
